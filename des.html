Extended Learning

Sometimes, deep focus is needed — whether it’s preparing thoroughly for upcoming exams, meeting tight deadlines on important assignments, or working through particularly challenging topics. In these moments, a little extra time can make all the difference.

  • Up to 15 minutes — Complimentary

    • Enjoy a grace period with no additional charge, letting your session flow naturally.

  • 16 to 30 minutes — 50% of your average session rate

    • For those moments when a little more focus makes all the difference.

  • Beyond 30 minutes — Full session fee

    • Extended time valued as a full additional session, to keep progress seamless and uninterrupted.

Seamless Payment Flexibility

At B. Miss Nanda, we believe in transparency and ease. When sessions extend, families have flexible ways to manage the extra time:

  • Pre-purchase monthly extended time credits to plan ahead effortlessly, ensuring uninterrupted focus.
  • Or, settle additional time at month-end through a simple, clear invoice.

Our pricing thoughtfully reflects the average rate of your chosen plan, designed to be fair and aligned with your child’s unique learning journey.
